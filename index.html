About Me

WEB DESIGNER AND GRAPHIC DESIGNER

I am Davis Kathungu. I create proffessional websites that get results. I am skilled in web design with over 3 years experience and graphics design with over 2 years experience. My passion lies in creating captivating website designs and implementing them through frontend development. I take pride in staying up to date with current designs, trends and leveraging my creativity to produce visually appealling and user friendly websites. Throughout my career i have developed a deep understanding of user expirience(UX) and user interface (UI) principles. I strive to create intuiive and seamless browsing experiences and believe that a well designed website should not only be visually appealling but also provide smooth and enjoyable interaction. When starting on a new project I throughly research and analyze the target audience and the clients specific requirements. This enables me to tailor my designs to meet their expectations and deliver a unique online presence that aligns with their brand identity...
